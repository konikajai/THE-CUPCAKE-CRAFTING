THE CUPCAKE CRAFTING INDIA

Australian Foods (I) Pvt. Ltd., the makers of THE CUPCAKE CRAFTING Cupcakes commenced its business operations in India in January 2000. The lack of recognized Cupcakes players in India motivated Australian Foods to enter the market and open its first store in Chennai in January 2000. The Cupcakes Crafting have rapidly grown to become synonymous with international quality and are today the #1 brand of fresh baked Cupcakes in the country.Currently, The Cupcakes Crafting operates 50 outlets in 17 cities in NCR, Gurgaon, Jaipur, Bhopal, Ahmedabad, Baroda, Surat, Mumbai, Pune, Chennai, Bangalore, Coimbatore, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Mangalore, Bhubaneshwar, Udaipur and Port Blair.
The Cupcake Crafting product line is unique, of high quality and good value for money. In India too, the Cupcakes are made from the finest ingredients available, ensuring that the products meet stringent global quality and consistency guidelines. All Cupcakes dough, toppings and related materials are produced in the Cookie Man Commissary in Chennai.
The Cupcake Crafting markets a diverse line of fresh baked Australian Cupcakes in India. There are more than fifty varieties of Cupcakes available. The flavours include Choc Chip, Coffee Walnut, Brandy Snap, Honey and Oats, Coconut Macaroon, Shortbread, Peanut, Ginger and many, many more. Even the famous Australian “Anzac” Cupcake is available right here in India.
Australian Foods has developed an efficient and successful franchising model that has been leveraged to increase operations in India at a rapid pace. Based on a shared passion for food retail, effective communication, we have forged successful partnerships with our franchisees across India.
